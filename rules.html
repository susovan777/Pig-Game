Pig Game Rules

1. To start the game first click on the 'ROLL DICE' button, it will roll random dice number which is calculated and shows in 'Current' section.

2. If you want to hold the current score then press on 'HOLD' button, it will save the score in current 'PLAYER' section and current player will change.

3. You can increase your score by rolling the dice many times but if the dice number shows 1 then Current score will reset and moved to next player.

4. Which player will reach 100 score he will be the winner of this game.

5. You can reset the game by click on 'NEW GAME' button.
